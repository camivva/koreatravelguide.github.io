When To Visit


South Korea has four distinct seasons to suit all types of travelers. But there is always one season that is everyone's favorite, which in this case is spring. However, the best time to visit Korea depends entirely on what you want to do there.

Best time to visit South Korea:

Spring, Mar-May

Spring is the best and most expected time in South Korea as the weather is generally warm and pleasant. And it is also the season when you can see dazzling pink cherry blossoms.

Summer, Jun-Aug

Summer is the least recommended season for travel. Summer in Korea is the rainy season when all of Korea gets hot and humid.

Autumn, Sept-Nov

Autumn comes with warm days and cold nights with beautiful landscapes coming alive by the colorful trees.

Winter, Dec-Feb

Winters in Korea are cold and snowy. Winter is perfect for snowboarding in the mountains and see historical temples covered in snow.

What To Pack?


Bringing a suitcase full of clothes or unnecessary things to Korea is a bad idea. There are many stores in every corner of Korea, and you will want to take advantage of each one of them. It is recommended to pack only the essentials. The following is a list of important and necessary things to pack.

  • Essential Documents- International driver's license, travel insurance documents, K-ETA, and Q-code documents.
  • Credit/ Debit Cards- Almost all stores accept credit cards (*Note* Call your bank company to let them know you will be using the card in Korea).
  • Medication- Korean medication may be different from what you need to have. So, it is recommended that you bring your own medication.
  • Electrical plug adapter- The voltage in Korea is 220V and the plugs are C and F type.
  • Personal hygiene products- The deodorant is very important to pack. Deodorant is hard to find in Korea.
  • Comfortable clothes and shoes- Depending on the season you are going to travel you have to pack clothes for the occasion.

Where To Stay in Korea?




South Korea has a variety of accommodation options. From five-star hotels to guesthouses. Accommodation prices depend on location and season.

Hotels

The cost of economy hotels starts from 38- 120 USD per night. Most hotels include basic amenities such as free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, TV, laundry, and electric teapot.

You can find low-cost hotels on the following pages:

  • Expedia
  • Booking

Renting

The cost of renting a shared space with private rooms starts from 18-100 USD. On the other hand, the rent for an entire property starts from 29-321 USD. Most affordable places offer basic amenities such as a kitchen, free Wi-Fi, TV, air conditioning, and free parking.

You can find stunning rental places of different price ranges from more expensive to more economical on the following page:

  • Airbnb

5-Star Luxury Hotels

Luxury Hotels stand out for the excellent quality of their equipment, facilities, decorations, and guest service. The cost of 5-star hotels can start from 436 USD and up. Luxury hotels offer basic amenities such as free Wi-Fi, TV, and air conditioning. And special amenities such as a spa, bar, restaurant, tennis court, babysitting, and much more.

Transportation



South Korea offers several transportation options which are buses, subways, cabs, ferries, and much more. Here are the best ways to get around in Korea.


Subway

Buses are the most popular in Korea, but the best way to get around the cities is by subway. Subway fares can be paid by transportation card or cash. A one-way pass costs about .95-1.02 USD and a day pass costs about 3.79 USD. Subways are available in major cities such as Seoul, Busan, Daegu, Gwangju, and Daejeon.

**Note** Subway fares depend on the region. The best option is to have a transportation card.


Bus

The bus system in Korea connects all corners of the cities with hundreds of routes running through each city. Buses usually take time but are cheaper and provide an easy way to get to any destination. Bus fares can be paid by transport card or cash.

**Note** Bus fares depend on the region. The best option is to have a transportation card.


Taxi

Taxis in Korea are inexpensive and easy to find anywhere. Korea offers different types of cabs: standard, delux, jumbo, and cabs that are specifically for tourists. Most cabs accept cash, credit cards, and transportation cards.

**Note** Taxi fares depend on the type of taxi, time of day, and length of trip.


For more information on transportation in Korea visit, Visit Korea.




- Transportation Cards -

In order to take any public transportation, it is most convenient to have transportation cards. There are different options of transportation cards and each one meets the specific needs of the traveler.

T-Money

T-money cards and Cashbee are transit cards that can be used on airport shuttles, public buses, subways, cabs, convenience stores, telephone booths, and package lockers. T-Money and Cashbee cards can be purchased at convenience stores such as GS25, 7-Eleven, and CU at a cost of 2,500 KRW (1.90 USD). The cards can be recharged at all subway and train stations.

Tourist Cards

South Korea offers numerous cards exclusively for tourists. The cards have a high price but they provide benefits not only in transportation but also for free entrance to popular attractions, discounts, and many more. Want to know more about these exclusive tourist cards? Visit, Visit Korea.

Best Apps To Use In Korea


There are many apps that you can use while traveling in Korea, but here is a list of essential apps for traveling in Korea that can make your trip easy and convenient.

Papago

Papago is a multilingual and fast translation app provided by Naver. The app offers amazing features such as image recognition. Papago's translation is accurate, so it is recommended to use this app during your trip to Korea.

Naver Map

Google Map does not work in Korea. In that case, Naver Map is the best application to use to get to your destination whether walking, by bus, subway, or driving. Naver Map is a Korean navigation application with an English version provided by Naver. The app offers navigation, routing, and public transportation information. The app allows you to save places you want to visit later.

Kakao Map

As Naver Map is the best application to use in Korea. Kakao Maps shows all available routes and public transportation schedules. The application allows you to save and share routes and places.

Subway Korea

Subway Korea is the best and easiest way to navigate around Korea. Subway Korea has an English version of the app and offers real-time transit information. The app allows you to save the most frequent routes and much more.

Mango Plate

Mango Plate is an app that helps you discover the best restaurants and cafes in Korea. Mango Plate offers a variety of information and offers an English version.

Visit Korea

Visit Korea an application developed by the Korea Tourism Organization. Visit Korea provides reliable information about Korea such as festivals, restaurants, attractions, accommodations, and much more.
